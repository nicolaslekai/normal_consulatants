how we work

Process / 3 phases
Phase 01 — 2 weeks

audit

We sit with your teams, map the real workflows, and find the places where AI removes cost or unlocks output. You get a written brief with numbers, not vibes.

Phase 02 — 4–8 weeks

build

We build the highest-value systems ourselves — automations, pipelines, assistants — in your stack, with your data, shipped in weekly increments you can veto.

Phase 03 — ongoing

embed

We train your people to run it, document everything, and step back. Optional retainer for new capability as the models change under everyone's feet.
